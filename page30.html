To the south, at the foot of the Vatican Hill, lay the Gardens of Nero and his circus, an earthen racecourse; only recently have its outlines been accurately traced. Another great showpiece along the Tiber, the Naumachia, built for mock sea battles, extended north of the Mausoleum of Hadrian; but all traces seem lost.
